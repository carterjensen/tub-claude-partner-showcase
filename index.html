Who We Are
A Human Training Company,
Powered by Claude

The Uncommon Business (TUB) is an AI education and business transformation company founded by Callan Faulkner. We don't just teach AI tools. We teach mindset, leadership, and opportunity recognition, with AI as the transformation vehicle.

Our flagship program, Automate to Accelerate (A2A), is a 12-week live cohort that takes business owners from "I use ChatGPT sometimes" to running a fully integrated AI operating system across every department of their business.

Claude is the backbone of everything we teach. From building AI employees with Claude Projects to deploying production-grade applications with Claude Code, our students learn to leverage Claude's full capability stack: Skills, Projects, the API, and agentic workflows.

Our team of 25 humans operates 150+ AI employees internally, practicing exactly what we teach. When we say Claude can transform a business, we're speaking from lived experience.

🎓

AI Architect Training

We coined the "AI Architect" identity. Students learn to Spot bottlenecks, Build AI employees to solve them, and Train those employees to improve over time.

⚡

Department-by-Department Methodology

Not random tool demos. Structured builds across Sales, Marketing, Operations, Finance, HR, and Customer Success. Each department gets its own Claude workspace.

Claude Projects, Claude Skills, Claude Code, MCP connectors, the Anthropic API. Our entire curriculum is built on and around Claude's ecosystem.

Why Claude
Claude Is Our Platform of Choice

We chose Claude because it's the best tool for building real business systems, and our students' results prove it.

01

Claude Projects as Department Workspaces

Every student builds five department workspaces using Claude Projects: Marketing, Sales, Customer Success, Operations, and Finance. Each workspace has custom instructions, a knowledge base, and department-specific AI employees.

02

Claude Skills for Repeatable Workflows

Students build Claude Skills that turn complex, multi-step business processes into one-click automations. From payroll processing to PR pitching to financial modeling, Skills are the backbone of their AI operating system.

03

Claude Code for Production Applications

Advanced students use Claude Code to build production-grade software: websites, chatbots, dashboards, and self-learning AI systems. Zero coding experience required. Claude Code is the co-builder.
