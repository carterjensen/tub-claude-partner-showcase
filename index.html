Who We Are
A human training company,
powered by Claude.

The Uncommon Business is an AI education and business transformation company founded by Callan Faulkner. We don't teach AI tools. We teach mindset, leadership, and opportunity recognition, with AI as the transformation vehicle.

Our flagship program, Automate to Accelerate (A2A), is a 12-week live cohort that takes business owners from casual AI use to running a fully integrated AI operating system across every department.

Claude is the backbone of everything we teach. From building department workspaces with Claude Projects to deploying production applications with Claude Code, our students learn to leverage the full capability stack: Skills, Projects, the API, and agentic workflows.

Our team of 25 humans operates 150+ AI employees internally, practicing exactly what we teach. When we say Claude transforms a business, we're speaking from lived experience.

01

AI Architect Identity

We coined the "AI Architect" identity. Students learn to Spot bottlenecks, Build AI employees to solve them, and Train those employees to improve over time.

02

Department-by-Department

Structured builds across Sales, Marketing, Operations, Finance, HR, and Customer Success. Each department gets its own Claude workspace with custom instructions and knowledge.

03

Claude-Native Curriculum

Claude Projects, Claude Skills, Claude Code, MCP connectors, the Anthropic API. Our entire curriculum is built on and around Claude's ecosystem.
